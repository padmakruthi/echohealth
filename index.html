About Echo Health

Echo Health is a specialized patient portal designed for the unique accessibility needs of seniors. By combining Computer Vision for pill identification and Natural Language Processing for native-language alerts, we minimize medication errors and promote independent living.

Help Center

How do I scan a pill?

Go to the 'Scan' section and point your camera at the tablet. Ensure there is good lighting for the AI to recognize the medicine.

Why are my alerts in Telugu?

The system automatically selects the language based on your profile (Mother uses Telugu, Father uses Hindi). Use 'Switch User' to change this.

What if the system is dark?

You can toggle Dark Mode using the moon/sun icon in the navigation bar at any time.
