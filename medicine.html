How it protects you

Our system uses real-time computer vision to extract pill names and dosages. This acts as a secondary validation layer, comparing your physical medication against your doctor's digital prescription record.

Help & Support

Pill not detected?

Ensure your medicine bottle is in a well-lit area and the name is clearly visible to the camera.

Wrong Medicine Alert?

If the alert is red, DO NOT take the medicine. Contact your doctor immediately.
